Questions

What counts as a document?
Every generated document counts — contracts, estimates, invoices, change orders, subcontractor agreements, lien waivers, and material takeoffs. Regenerating an existing document counts as a new generation.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your Settings page and your plan stays active through the end of the billing period. No questions asked.
What's the difference between Pro and Business?
Business is built for contractors running multiple crews or managing projects in multiple states. It includes multi-user support and priority support — coming soon.
Do you store my documents?
Yes. All generated documents are saved to your account and accessible from your dashboard. They're yours to download, email, or re-open any time.
